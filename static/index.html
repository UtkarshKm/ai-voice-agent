Your personal voice-activated AI assistant.

Engage in natural, spoken conversations with a powerful AI that can search, learn, and respond in a human-like voice. Dhwani is your partner for brainstorming, learning, and exploration.

API Key Requirements

To use Dhwani, you'll need to provide your own API keys from the following services. These keys are stored exclusively in your browser and are never sent to our servers.

  • Google Gemini (For conversational AI)
  • AssemblyAI (For speech-to-text)
  • Murf.ai (For text-to-speech)
  • Tavily (For web search capabilities)
